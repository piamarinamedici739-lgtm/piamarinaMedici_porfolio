About Me

I am a 4th-year BS Information Technology student with academic and practical experience in data handling, data analytics, and introductory machine learning. I have hands-on experience experimenting with ML models, including training and fine-tuning existing models for domain-specific tasks.

In our thesis project, MandaCare: A Complaint Analysis System for the City Health Department of Mandaluyong City, I served as both the front-end developer and the machine learning developer. I worked specifically on the system’s automatic complaint categorization and urgency detection using sentiment analysis.

I also possess fundamental skills in Figma prototyping and UI/UX design, coupled with practical experience in user interface development. Additionally, I completed a Basic AI Workshop under AI Singapore, which strengthened my foundational knowledge in artificial intelligence.
