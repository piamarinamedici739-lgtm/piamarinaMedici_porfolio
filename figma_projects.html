RoomEase: Smart Reservation System

+
Project Overview & Scenario

Role: Lead UI/UX Designer  |  Client: Jose Rizal University (Concept)

The Real-Life Problem: In a busy university setting, booking a laboratory or lecture hall is often a logistical nightmare. Faculty members frequently face double-booking conflicts, students wander around looking for available study spaces, and the administrative staff relies on manual logbooks that are prone to errors.

The Smart Solution: RoomEase digitizes this entire process. It provides real-time visibility of room availability, allowing users to book instantly from their devices. It eliminates schedule conflicts through an automated approval system.
